How To Use The APP?

Download and Install

Download and Install Callpay app in your android smartphones and smart devices
from google play. It is a free to use Application; you don’t have to pay a rupee to use
Callpay from your Smart phones, Tablets, Laptops or Computers.

Register

Register yourself with your mobile no.
and other details like user name, password,
E - mail address etc.. Remember, all your
information is safe, secured and end-to-end encrypted.

Categories

Select your Categories – you will be shown various categories of ads like
entertainment, shopping, utilities etc.. You select more ad categories, you get
more ads, means more money for you.

Advertisement

You receive an Advertisement during and after every
call you receive, just click the ad after the call and
follow the steps to download, install or register.

Paytm Wallet

Each install, register, and clicks you make; it fills your Paytm Wallet
with Indian Rupee. Which is completely redeemable and can be
used for purchasing anything.

Withdraw Cash

You can withdraw your cash in the batches of INR 50 or 100
to your PayTM Wallet.

How To Redeem?

Step 1

Each install, register, and clicks you make; earns you points, converts to Indian Rupee in E-Wallet. You can see your earned amount in your E-wallet.

Step 2

You can withdraw your cash in the batches of INR 50 or 100 to your PayTM Wallet.
